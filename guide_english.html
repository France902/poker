🎰 Basic Poker Rules (Texas Hold'em)

🃎 1. Objective

👉 Make the best 5-card hand possible and/or get others to fold.

📦 2. Setup

  • Played with a 52-card deck
  • Minimum 2 players, max 10
  • Each player gets 2 face-down cards

🔁 3. Game Phases

  • Pre-Flop – You get 2 cards, first betting round
  • Flop – 3 community cards are revealed, betting continues
  • Turn – 1 more card added, next betting round
  • River – Last card revealed, final bets
  • Showdown – Time to reveal cards and find the winner

💰 4. Possible Actions

  • Check = Pass without betting (if allowed)
  • Bet = Place a bet
  • Raise = Increase the current bet
  • Fold = Give up and throw your cards

🏆 5. Hand Rankings

  • Royal Flush (10-J-Q-K-A of the same suit)
  • Four of a Kind (4 cards of the same rank)
  • Full House (Three of a kind + a pair)
  • Flush (5 cards of the same suit)
  • Straight (5 cards in sequence)
  • Three of a Kind
  • Two Pair
  • One Pair
  • High Card (if you got nothin' else)
